The Rise of 'K' in US Baby Names

-Initial of names that have increased the most since 1880-

By Masashi Ijichi

The initials of the name that most increased its presence in just over 140 years was K. According to the U.S. Social Security Administration, the percentage of names beginning with the letter K accounted for 9.2% in 2023, up 8.1 percentage points from 1880, when the statistics began. The initially popular name was Kathleen, which was gradually replaced by Kenneth and Kevin. Currently, the most common name is Kai.
